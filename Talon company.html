Welcome to Talon Company

Talon Company is an elite subdivision within the 327th Star Corps, dedicated to excellence, discipline, and teamwork in the Star Wars Roblox universe. As part of the Grand Army of the Republic, we uphold the values of loyalty, courage, and tactical superiority in every operation.

Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a new recruit, Talon Company offers a structured and immersive experience in a milsim (military simulation) environment.

About Talon Company

Talon Company operates under the 327th Star Corps, specializing in:

  • Frontline Combat – Engage in large-scale battles across iconic Star Wars locations.
  • Specialized Training – Hone your skills in marksmanship, tactics, and teamwork.
  • Structured Ranks – Progress through our ranks based on merit and dedication.
  • Community Events – Participate in operations, trainings, and social gatherings.

Our goal is to provide a fun, organized, and immersive experience for all members.
